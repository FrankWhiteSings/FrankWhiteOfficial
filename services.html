Welcome

Frank White is a soul-driven Rhythm and Blues vocalist whose music speaks to love, life, and lived experience.

With a warm second-tenor range and a delivery rooted in classic soul, his performances blend emotional storytelling with timeless musicianship.

Whether performing intimate venues or larger stages, Frank delivers music that connects across generations.

White is a powerful vocalist whose mission is to spread love, emotion, and soul through every note. His voice is built for the stage, television, and hearts around the world.

What to Expect When Booking Frank White

When you book Frank White, you’re not just hiring a singer — you’re inviting an unforgettable experience. Expect a soulful, heartfelt performance that connects with your audience. Frank arrives prepared, professional, and fully committed to making your event special. From intimate gatherings to grand stages, every performance is tailored to create lasting memories. His resilience, passion, and authenticity shine through every note.
