Welcome to the Gateway Experience for Grown Folks

Frank White is a Bronx-born, Philadelphia-based vocalist whose soul-driven voice delivers raw emotion, balance, and truth. Balance is key as an October Libra and dedicated single father, Frank pours love, resilience, and lived experience into every performance.

His mission is simple but powerful: to inspire love through music and bring authentic soul to global stages like AGT, The Voice, and American Idol. With a warm second-tenor range and a delivery rooted in classic soul, his performances blend emotional storytelling with timeless musicianship. Whether performing in intimate venues or on larger stages, Frank connects deeply with audiences across generations.

Every note Frank sings is built to move hearts and leave a lasting impression. Balancing his passion with life as a single dad, he embodies dedication, authenticity, and determination in every performance.
